## The Twitch-to-TikTok Conversion Formula

### Step 1: Extract the Raw Moment

**Ideal clip length for TikTok:**
* **15-30 seconds:** Perfect for most content
* **30-45 seconds:** Educational or story-based
* **45-60 seconds:** Epic montages or compilations

**What to include:**
* **2-3 seconds** before the main moment (build-up)
* **The main event** (peak moment)
* **2-3 seconds** after (payoff/reaction)

### Step 2: Reframe for Vertical

**Twitch streams are 16:9. TikTok is 9:16.**

**Smart reframing strategies:**

**Face cam priority:**
* Keep your face cam in frame
* Ensure reactions are visible
* Center facial expressions

**Action focus:**
* Track important gameplay elements
* Keep kill feed visible
* Maintain UI readability

**Dynamic cropping:**
* Move frame focus as action moves
* Zoom in on critical moments
* Pull out for full context when needed

**Don't just crop center:**
* Manually adjust for each moment
* Prioritize what tells the story
* Test readability on mobile

---

### Step 3: Add TikTok-Specific Enhancements

**Subtitles are mandatory:**
* 80% watch without sound
* Emphasize punchlines
* Highlight key words
* Use contrasting colors

**Text overlays:**
* Set up context in first frame
* Explain what's about to happen
* Add commentary on your gameplay
* Include call-to-actions

**Trending audio:**
* Replace stream audio with trending sounds (when appropriate)
* Keep original audio for authentic reactions
* Use music for montages
* Sync moments to beat drops

**Visual effects:**
* Zoom-ins on key moments
* Slow-motion on epic plays
* Replay from different angles
* On-screen graphics and arrows

---

### Step 4: Hook in 3 Seconds

**Your first 3 seconds must:**

**Create intrigue:**
* "Wait for it..."
* "This should be impossible..."
* "Watch what happens next..."

**Show the payoff first:**
* Start with the highlight
* Then show how you got there
* Context comes after hook

**Use visual hooks:**
* Fast cuts to create energy
* Unexpected first frame
* Text that promises value

**Examples:**

‚ùå **Bad:** Stream slowly, gradually build to moment
‚úÖ **Good:** Show epic moment, then rewind to setup

‚ùå **Bad:** Long intro explaining context
‚úÖ **Good:** Jump to action with text context overlay

## Avoiding Common Mistakes

### Mistake #1: Posting Horizontal Videos

**Problem:** Black bars, unreadable UI, poor mobile experience
**Solution:** Always reframe to 9:16 vertical

### Mistake #2: No Subtitles

**Problem:** Lose 80% of potential viewers
**Solution:** Add clear, readable subtitles to every video

### Mistake #3: Too Long

**Problem:** Viewer drop-off, algorithm penalty
**Solution:** Keep it under 45 seconds when possible

### Mistake #4: No Hook

**Problem:** Viewers scroll past in first second
**Solution:** Lead with the payoff, context second

### Mistake #5: Inconsistent Posting

**Problem:** Algorithm doesn't favor sporadic posters
**Solution:** Post daily, batch record/edit to maintain pace

### Mistake #6: Ignoring Trends

**Problem:** Missing algorithm boosts from trending audio
**Solution:** Monitor trends, participate with gaming twist
