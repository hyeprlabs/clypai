## The Creative Friction

We’ve all felt it. You look at a finished podcast or a marathon stream, knowing there are gold nuggets buried inside, but the thought of digging them out feels like a mountain you aren’t ready to climb. 

You want to share your best moments, but instead, you’re staring at a timeline, bogged down by the friction of manual editing. This is where the spark of creativity usually goes to die: **in the tedious middle ground between recording and posting.**

While we crave the reach of short-form video, the process of getting there is broken. Scrubbing through hours of footage just to find one punchline drains the energy you should be using to create. You get lost in the mechanics—syncing subtitles, repositioning frames, and hunting for the right emoji—until the joy of sharing your message is replaced by the fatigue of the "grind."
